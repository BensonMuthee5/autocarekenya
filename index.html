who are we

Manufacturing of all types of rigid bodies, tippers, drop side trailers, canter bodies , and much more according to the clients specifications

We also refurbish prime movers and trailers to new and supply the following brands ranging from BEIBEN ,MERCEDES, HOWO & FAW trucks.
